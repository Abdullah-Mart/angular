Berry Poffin Recipe

Ingredients:

Amount of servings:

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Berries:

    Wash and chop the berries into small pieces.

  2. Mix the Dry Ingredients:

    In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. Combine Wet Ingredients:

    In another bowl, mix the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.

  4. Make the Batter:

    Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.

    Fold in the chopped berries.

  5. Bake the Poffins:

    Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).

    Grease a muffin tin and fill each cup with the batter.

    Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  6. Cool and Serve:

    Let the Poffins cool for a few minutes before removing them from the tin.

    Serve them as a delightful treat for your Pokémon-themed party!
